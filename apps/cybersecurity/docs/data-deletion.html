DCI Cybersecurity Labs — Delete Your Data

Last updated: March 2026

What data does DCI Cybersecurity Labs store?

DCI Cybersecurity Labs stores all user data locally on your device. This includes completed labs, scores, XP, streaks, and learning progress. No personal information (name, email, phone number) is collected or stored.

DCI Cybersecurity Labs also uses Firebase Anonymous Authentication, which generates a random anonymous identifier (UID) on your device. This UID is not linked to any personal information.

How to delete your data

You can delete all of your DCI Cybersecurity Labs data directly from the app:

1 Open DCI Cybersecurity Labs
2 Tap the Settings tab (bottom navigation)
3 Tap Reset Progress
4 Confirm by tapping Reset Everything

This will permanently delete all locally stored progress data, including completed labs, scores, XP, and streak history. This action cannot be undone.

What gets deleted

All progress data stored in the app's local storage, including: completed lab records, best scores, attempt history, XP totals, XP history, streak counts, and scenario results.

What is not affected

The anonymous Firebase UID will be regenerated the next time you use the app. Uninstalling the app will also remove all locally stored data and the Firebase UID.
